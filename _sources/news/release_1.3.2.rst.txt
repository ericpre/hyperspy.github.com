HyperSpy 1.3.2  has been released!
==================================

.. feed-entry::
   :date: 2018-7-10

We are pleased to announce the release of HyperSpy 1.3.2


This is a maintenance release. Follow the following links for details on all
the `bugs fixed
<https://github.com/hyperspy/hyperspy/issues?q=milestone%3Av1.3.2+label%3A%22type%3A+bug%22>`_
and `enhancements <https://github.com/hyperspy/hyperspy/issues?utf8=%E2%9C%93&q=milestone%3Av1.3.2+label%3A%22type%3A+enhancement%22+>`_.

Note: since v1.3.1 the HyperSpy WinPython Bundle distribution is
not released in sync with HyperSpy. For HyperSpy WinPython Bundle
releases see https://github.com/hyperspy/hyperspy-bundle
